About Namaste Tools

Our Story

Namaste Tools was founded in 2023 by Abhishek Agrahari with a vision to create the most advanced, user-friendly PDF toolkit available. Frustrated by the limitations of existing PDF tools, Abhishek set out to build a solution that combined powerful features with an intuitive interface.

Today, Namaste Tools serves millions of users worldwide, helping students, professionals, and businesses streamline their document workflows. Our AI-powered tools save countless hours by automating complex tasks and delivering professional results with just a few clicks.

We're committed to continuous innovation, regularly adding new features and improving existing ones based on user feedback. Our mission is to make PDF management effortless, accessible, and secure for everyone.
